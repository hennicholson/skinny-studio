## Key Features

**Chat-Based Creation**
Talk to your AI Creative Director like you would a real collaborator. Describe your vision, refine it through conversation, and generate exactly what you imagine.

**Multiple AI Models**
Access the world's most powerful image and video generation models—Flux Pro, Seedream, Kling, Minimax, and more—all in one place.

**Smart Model Selection**
Not sure which AI model to use? Your Creative Director analyzes your request and automatically selects the perfect model for the job.

**Personal Library**
Every creation is saved to your library. Organize with folders, search by prompt, and build your personal collection of AI-generated assets.

**Skills System**
Save your favorite styles, techniques, and prompts as reusable "Skills" that you can apply to any future generation with a single tap.

**Creator Gallery**
Get inspired by featured community creations and discover what's possible.

**Image-to-Video**
Upload any image and transform it into cinematic video content.

**Reference Images**
Attach images to guide your generations—perfect for style matching, product shots, or character consistency.
